EPILOGUE

Epilogue

The story so far, to be continued.

Every project began long before the first line of code.

It began in quiet afternoons that passed unnoticed, in late nights where one stubborn bug refused to give up, and in countless moments where quitting would have been easier than trying again.

This portfolio isn't just a collection of projects.

It's a collection of questions that slowly found their answers, mistakes that quietly became lessons, and a curiosity that never stopped asking, "What if I try one more time?"

Every line of code carries a version of me that knew a little less than the one writing this today.

For Mom and Dad...

Before there was code, there was a home where curiosity was allowed to grow.

Thank you for believing in me long before I had anything to prove.

Every opportunity I've been fortunate enough to chase began with your patience, your sacrifices, and your quiet faith that I could become more than I imagined for myself.

No achievement on this website belongs to me alone.

Every one carries a piece of you.

For every teacher...

The lessons I remember most weren't always written in textbooks.

Sometimes they were questions that stayed with me long after class had ended.

Sometimes they were words of encouragement at exactly the right time.

Thank you for reminding me that learning isn't about knowing every answer—it's about never being afraid to keep asking.

For my friends...

Thank you for the conversations that had nothing to do with programming, yet somehow made every project a little easier to return to.

For the laughter after long days.

For the encouragement you probably don't even remember giving.

The journey would have looked very different without those moments.

To everyone on the internet who shared what they knew...

A tutorial.

A GitHub repository.

A Stack Overflow answer.

A page hidden somewhere deep in the documentation.

You answered questions for people you'll probably never meet.

I was one of them.

Thank you for choosing to share what you knew instead of keeping it to yourself.

To ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, and every AI that helped me learn...

You never wrote my story for me.

You never felt the frustration of chasing the same bug for hours, the doubt that came with it, or the joy of finally seeing everything work.

Those moments belonged to me.

But every time I felt stuck, you were there with another explanation, another perspective, and another reason to keep trying.

You didn't walk the journey for me.

You simply made sure I never had to face it without a way forward.

Sometimes, that's all someone needs.

To my younger self...

The kid who opened QBASIC without realizing he had just taken the first step.

You never imagined Java.

Or Scaccomatto.

Or Alekhine Studios.

Or dreams of studying Cyber Security at ITMO University.

You only wanted to understand how things worked.

Looking back, maybe that was enough.

Every journey begins with curiosity.

And to you...

Thank you for taking the time to read my story.

What you've seen isn't the destination.

It's simply a snapshot of one chapter in a journey that's still unfolding.

Years from now, I hope these projects feel small.

I hope this portfolio feels unfinished.

Because that would mean I never stopped learning, never stopped building, and never stopped wondering what comes next.

Perhaps the next chapter... is one we build together.
